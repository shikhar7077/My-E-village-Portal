Welcome to My e-village

My e-Village is an innovative project that aims to connect rural communities through a comprehensive website platform. It provides a digital space where villagers can showcase their development initiatives, share information about schools and gram panchayat activities, and access donations for community development from urban cities and government sources.

    My E-village is project that connects rural cummunities

    My e-Village is an innovative project that aims to connect rural communities through a comprehensive website platform

    The My e-Village project consists of the following key components:

    • User-Friendly Website
    • Development Showcases
    • School Activities Hub
    • Gram Panchayat Updates
    • Donation Portal
    • Online Forums and Collaboration Spaces

    villagers to create profiles

    Develop an intuitive and user-friendly website that allows villagers to create profiles, share updates, and access relevant information easily.

    Development Showcases

    Enable villagers to showcase their development initiatives, such as infrastructure projects, health and sanitation programs, skill development initiatives, and more.

    School activities hub

    Provide a dedicated space for schools to share their activities, achievements, educational initiatives, and requirements, encouraging collaboration and support.

    Gram Panchayat Updates

    Offer a platform for gram panchayats to share information about their decisions, projects, budgets, and progress, fostering transparency and citizen engagement.

    Donation Portal

    Create a secure and transparent donation portal where urban citizens, NGOs, and government agencies can contribute funds for specific development projects identified by the villagers.

    Collaboration Spaces

    Establish online forums and collaboration spaces where villagers can engage in discussions, seek advice, and share best practices, promoting knowledge exchange and learning.
